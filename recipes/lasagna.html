Lasagna

Lasagna, or lasagne, is a classic Italian dish consisting of wide, flat sheets of pasta layered with fillings such as ragù (meat sauce), béchamel sauce, vegetables, and various cheeses (including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan). The assembled dish is topped with cheese and baked (known as al forno)

Steps to mkae Lasgna

  1. Cook noodles (if not using no-boil).
  2. Brown meats and sauté aromatics (onion, garlic).
  3. Simmer tomatoes and seasonings to create the sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and parsley for the cheese layer.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella and bake until hot and bubbly.
